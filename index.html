What is NASA Space Apps?

NASA Space Apps Challenge is an international hackathon that engages thousands of citizens across the globe to use NASA's open data to create innovative solutions to challenges we face on Earth and in space.

For 48 hours each year, participants from around the world come together at hundreds of simultaneous local events to solve challenges submitted by NASA experts. Teams of technologists, scientists, designers, entrepreneurs, artists, and others collaborate to answer some of the most pressing challenges on Earth and in space.

Why Participate?

  • Work with NASA's open data to create solutions to real-world problems
  • Collaborate with a global community of innovators
  • Develop skills in coding, design, storytelling, and more
  • Showcase talents to NASA and space agency experts
  • Win awards and global recognition

How It Works

  • Find a Local Event: Join one of hundreds of local events around the world
  • Form a Team: Work solo or collaborate with others (teams of up to 6)
  • Choose a Challenge: Select from NASA-proposed challenges across multiple themes
  • Build Your Solution: Use NASA data and resources to create your project
  • Submit Your Project: Present your solution before judges at your local event
